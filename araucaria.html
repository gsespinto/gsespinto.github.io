ARAUCÁRIA

'Araucária' is a virtual reality experience for Google Cardboard, in which you follow a short narrative. In it you take on the role of a child, talking to her mother about deforestation and sustainability, while you interpret the point of view of a bird and a bee with an arcady gameplay.

The project was made by a group of three, of which I was the team's programmer. I wrote the code base of the project and dealt with most of the aspects within the Unity Engine.

My biggest take away from the project was the code's optimization for mobile. It was achieved by placing as little code as possible on the tick functions , and relying on event callbacks. This also allowed us to make modular components for the different objects in the game, and increase code reusability.

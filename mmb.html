MMB

'Mixed Martial Beats' is a 2.5D beat ‘em up platformer computer game taking place in a post apocalyptic world, in which the human race is no more and animals have evolved and adapted to live among the ruins of the old world. There's a lot of abilities associated with movement like dash, double jump, wall jump and slide. The combat is based on melee combos and ranged powers. The player has to escape from an enemy base where he was held captive.

​In this project I did all the programming and participated in the game and sound design. The goal of this college project was to make a 2D game, but since our team had 3D artists we decided to make the game's playability fully 2D falling into the assignments criteria, but the visuals are a mixture of 2D and 3D assets.

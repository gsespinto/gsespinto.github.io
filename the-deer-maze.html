THE DEER MAZE

A small group project made for my master's degree, in which we explored the Unreal Engine in two weeks. I was the team's programmer and since I had experience with the game engine, I decided to program the foundation of the game in C++, and move on to blueprints as time constraints got tighter.
The Deer Maze is a small simple game in which the player has to find the end of a mysterious maze, fetching keys and lore items along the way.

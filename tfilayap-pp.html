Introduction

Welcome to "The Floor Is Lava And You Are Paper" (the "Game"). This privacy policy (the "Policy") explains how we handle your information when you use our Game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or share any personal data from users. This includes, but is not limited to:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Payment information
- Contact lists

2. Information We Do Collect

We do not collect any user data. The Game does not require you to provide any personal information, and it does not access or gather any information from your device beyond what is necessary to operate the Game.

3. Game Data

All game-related data is stored locally on your device. This includes:
- Game progress
- Items acquired
- Game settings
This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party services that collect data from users.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, we do not need to implement data security measures for protecting such data. The game data stored on your device is subject to the security measures provided by your device's operating system and hardware.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Game is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete the information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically for any changes.

THE FLOOR IS LAVA
AND YOU ARE PAPER

This is a small project I started to freshen up on Unity and C# before college re-opened. It's really unpolished but it served its purpose. Besides the academic goal, I just wanted to make a fun and challeging game, while exploring some implementation methods.

The game is just about reaching the end of the level before the time runs out and avoiding falling to the lava filled floor. Along the way you can catch some paper boats to then exchange them for new skins.

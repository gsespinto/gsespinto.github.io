A TURTLE'S JOURNEY

A simple game developed for the Gamers4Nature 2020 gamejam. The theme was 'microplastics', so I decided to make a game on the everyday struggle of ocean turtles.

The game as two modes:
- Infinite Runner
- Infinite Evader

Runner - The player controls the vertical movement of the turtle as it travels endlessly to the right. The objective is to go as far as possible while avoiding the garbage coming in the opposite direction.
Evader - The player has complete control over the turtle's movement. The goal is to avoid the garbage coming from all directions to survive as long as possible.

Six months later I picked up the project again and decided to clean it up and add features to post it in the google playstore. I really learned a lot from this project, being the first time I fully solo developed a game and the first time doing it for mobile. My main take away was learning mobile frameworks and how to publish a game on the playstore. Currently the app is in open tests, avaiable in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada and Portugal. You could say that this is my passion project.
